How it works

Natural voice interface means there’s no learning curve. Elders just talk to Grancentral’s friendly robot avatar.

Easy to set up and use, with nothing to wear, nothing to charge, just an attractive base station that can be placed anywhere you’d put a picture frame.

Simple two-way video messaging makes it easier for the elder and their family to stay connected in spite of busy schedules or timezone challenges.

Grancentral learns elders' daily activities and uses a mobile app to alert caregivers about changes that need attention.

Conversational screening and machine learning unobtrusively monitor elders’ mental and physical well-being.
